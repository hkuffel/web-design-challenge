Temperature By Latitude

This plot measures the temperature (in Fahrenheit) of each city in the dataset in relation to its latitude. The color of each marker also reflects its temperature, with the red dots naturally representing warmer temperatures than the blue dots. As you can clearly see, temperatures increase as one approaches the Equator.
